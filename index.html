Research

Our research delves into the fundamental properties of both materials and plasmas, and the complex interactions between them across a wide range of applications. We also investigate material behavior independently of plasma, including optoelectronic properties, resistive switching, and functional responses under external stimuli. To capture these processes across scales, we use a combination of atomistic simulations at both quantum and classical levels, as well as particle-based and continuum models. Through this multiscale, multiphysics approach — and in close collaboration with experimental partners — we aim to address challenges in health, sustainability, advanced electronics, and nanofabrication.
