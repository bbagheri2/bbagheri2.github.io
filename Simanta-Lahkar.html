Simanta Lahkar

Simanta received his Bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering and integrated Bachelor’s and Master’s Degree in Physics from Birla Institute of Technology and Science, India, in 2018. He graduated with his PhD from Shanghai Jiao Tong University, China, in 2022, and worked as an Early Career Fellow in Indian Institute of Technology – Gandhinagar, India, before joining TU/e in 2023. His main research interests are in the fields of computational physics and modeling, electron beam metrology, chemical vapor deposition of 2D materials, and device physics.
